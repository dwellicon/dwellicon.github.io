About Us

Founded in early 2024, Dwellicon was born from a frustrating weekend of endless real estate searches. Our founders, Alexandra Rodriguez and James Park, spent hours clicking through dozens of websites, opening countless tabs, and losing track of promising properties in the chaos.

"There has to be a better way," Alex said to James over coffee that Sunday afternoon. They realized that while AI was transforming every industry, house hunting was still stuck in the stone age of manual browsing.

Six months later, Dwellicon launched with a simple mission: let AI do the heavy lifting so you can focus on what matters—finding your dream home. Today, we've helped over 50,000 users discover their perfect property, saving them countless hours and endless frustration.

We're a Corporation (C-Corp)

Dwellicon operates as a C-Corporation, which means we're a separate legal entity from our founders. This structure provides you with critical protections and benefits:

Your Data is Protected: As a corporation, we maintain strict liability separation. Your personal information, search history, and payment details are protected by corporate safeguards, not tied to individual owners who could face personal legal issues.

Long-Term Stability: Unlike sole proprietorships that dissolve when owners leave, Dwellicon continues operating regardless of management changes. Your saved searches, favorite properties, and account history remain secure for years to come.

Investment in Innovation: Our corporate structure allows us to raise capital from investors, which means we can continuously improve our AI, expand our property database, and add features you request—without raising your subscription prices.

Our Agile Leadership Style

We embrace an agile, user-first leadership approach that directly benefits you:

Rapid Response to Your Needs: Our flat organizational structure means when you suggest a feature in the morning, our team can have it in development by afternoon. No bureaucracy, no endless approval chains—just solutions.

Transparent Communication: Our product roadmap is public, our response times are tracked, and every team member has direct access to user feedback. You know exactly what we're building and why.

Empowered AI: Just as we empower our team to make quick decisions, our AI is empowered to learn from your behavior and adapt to your preferences instantly—giving you better results with every search.
