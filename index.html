About Us

Founded in early 2024, Dwellicon was born from a frustrating weekend of endless real estate searches. Our founders, Alexandra Rodriguez and James Park, spent hours clicking through dozens of websites, opening countless tabs, and losing track of promising properties in the chaos.

"There has to be a better way," Alex said to James over coffee that Sunday afternoon. They realized that while AI was transforming every industry, house hunting was still stuck in the stone age of manual browsing.

Six months later, Dwellicon launched with a simple mission: let AI do the heavy lifting so you can focus on what matters—finding your dream home. Today, we've helped over 50,000 users discover their perfect property, saving them countless hours and endless frustration.

Our Leadership Philosophy

Image: Leadership (image2.png)

At Dwellicon, we embrace an agile, user-first leadership approach. Every decision we make starts with a simple question: "Does this make house hunting easier for our users?"

How This Benefits You:

Our flat organizational structure means rapid innovation. When users suggest a feature in the morning, our team can have it in development by afternoon. No bureaucracy, no endless approval chains—just solutions.

We practice radical transparency. Our product roadmap is public, our response times are tracked, and every team member has direct access to user feedback. This creates a culture where your needs drive our priorities, not the other way around.

Most importantly, we empower our AI with the same values. Our algorithms prioritize your preferences, learn from your feedback, and adapt to your unique search style—because the best leadership knows when to listen.

Want or Need? Both.

Meet Our Team

Alexandra Rodriguez

Co-Founder & CEO

Alexandra brings 12 years of experience in AI and machine learning from her time at Google and Tesla. She holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford, where her research focused on natural language processing and recommendation systems. After experiencing the pain of house hunting firsthand, she left her role as Senior ML Engineer to build Dwellicon. Her vision is simple: make AI accessible and useful for life's biggest decisions.

James Park

Co-Founder & CTO

James is a full-stack engineer and product designer with a passion for elegant user experiences. He earned his degree in Software Engineering from MIT and spent 8 years building consumer products at Airbnb and Zillow, giving him unique insight into the real estate tech space. At Dwellicon, he oversees all technical architecture and product design, ensuring our platform is both powerful and intuitive. His motto: "If it takes more than three clicks, we've failed."
